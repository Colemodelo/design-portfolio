Michael "coleModelo" Rudloff

Hi! My name is Michael Rudloff otherwise known as "coleModelo." I'm a full-time freelance web developer based out of Denver, CO. Since an early age, I've always been curious about design patterns and technology. Over the years, that curiousity has grown into a love for digital media and the world wide web.

While I do not do full-time graphic design, it frequently overlaps with my work as a web developer. This had allowed me to transfer my skills as a graphic designer into web development. In this portfolio, I've tried to curate a wide range of the work I've completed over the years that best represents my skills as a designer.
